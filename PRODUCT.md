# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Engineering leads and senior reviewers validating AI-generated changes before merge. They are focused, time-constrained, and need to know whether green CI actually proves the behavior promised by an issue.

## Product Purpose

GapWitness turns a change request, diff, tests, and CI output into an evidence graph. It identifies unsupported behavioral claims and generates a runnable counterexample so a reviewer can replace intuition with inspectable proof. Success is a complete no-login judge flow that finds the hidden concurrency gap and reproduces it in under three minutes.

## Brand Personality

Forensic, calm, exact. GapWitness is confident about evidence and explicit about uncertainty. It never uses alarmist AI language or claims more than the artifacts prove.

## Anti-references

No chatbot shell, generic AI dashboard, neon terminal aesthetic, glassmorphism, decorative metrics, tiled card grid, or dramatic security theater. Do not imitate third-party branding or display third-party logos.

## Design Principles

1. Show the chain of proof, not a score.
2. Make every conclusion inspectable at the source line.
3. Let the counterexample be the memorable moment.
4. Keep the reviewer in control without adding workflow friction.
5. Label live, cached, replayed, and unverified evidence honestly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard-only operation, visible focus, semantic landmarks, screen-reader progress announcements, reduced motion, 44px touch targets where practical, and status cues that never rely on color alone.
